Fusion Surveillance

Perimeter intrusion detection - AI based vision system for a secure perimeter A new age Perimeter Intrusion Detection System (PIDS) is a state-of-the-art AI based vision technology system eliminating the false positive alerts of the traditional fiber and infrared applications.

An AI solution with 100% accuracy in detecting the human intrusion coupling with scene detection gives our customers the right solution to protect their perimeter better & efficiently.

Applicable to literally to any type of perimeter be it concrete wall, fence, barbed wire, stone compounds or even rapidly deployable temporary perimeters, Perimetrion works well with all these environments with ease.

Built on the neural compute engine platform, Perimetrion comes with integrated dashboard with zoning, voice alerts, hooter and an optional inbuilt LED smart lighting illuminating the perimeter at night.

Our Goal

  • Vision systems based on AI & Deep Learning for accurate breach & alerts
  • Combination of thermal & Visual surveillance system integrated into one
  • GPU neural compute engine platform for quicker & efficient preventive actioning
  • In-camera IVA such as intrusion detection, ROI, Zoning, multilingual voice alerts, motion triggered lighting, profile segregation, scene detection
  • GPU neural compute engine platform for quicker & efficient preventive actioning
  • Integrated dashboard at central command center and vBots apps for instant alerts and actioning
  • Eliminating complete false positives with a clear profile segregation only for human presence and scene detection
  • User customisable intensity of capture & detection of breach
  • Works in isolation even if the network is broken as its in-camera inference solution
  • Anti-camera tampering alerts for disconnection & blind fold
  • Inbuilt hooter for automated alarms and voice alerts as a first level deterrent
  • Unique IP66 enclosure with inbuilt smart LED lighting and vandal-proof features
  • Modular lens format instead of an open camera design giving the camouflaged edge
  • Heart beat sensory for component level auto-diagnostics alerts to maintenance dashboard for a smooth 24/7 functioning
  • Integrates multiple sites onto a unified platform for centralised monitoring & command
  • Works with different types of perimeters such as concrete compounds, stone walls, fenced perimeter, barbed wire enclosures and even rapidly deployable temporary perimeter or enclosures
